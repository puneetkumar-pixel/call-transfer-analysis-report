Feb 9 — Key Recommendations

1
Fix Call Drops Critical

16 calls (35.6% of non-transfers) lost to technical issues. Many had positive sentiment. Fixing this alone could increase transfers from 15 to ~22 — a 47% improvement.

2
Lead Deduplication Critical

12 customers (26.7%) had already been contacted. Improve CRM sync to avoid redundant calls.

3
Add Callback Scheduling High

4 customers were busy. AI should offer to call back at a convenient time instead of losing the lead.

4
Lead Freshness Filtering High

3 customers had cancelled their move. Filter out stale/cancelled leads before dialing.

5
AI Disclosure Strategy Medium

1 customer disconnected after learning it's AI. Consider better disclosure or faster transfer for AI-skeptical customers.

Feb 9 — Bottom Line

Of 600 calls initiated, 490 were answered (81.7%). 295 hit voicemail (60.2%). Of the remaining 195 non-voicemail calls, 60 customers engaged but only 15 were transferred to a human agent — a 3.1% effective transfer rate. The #1 issue is call drops (16 calls, 35.6%) — many positive-sentiment customers lost to technical failures. The #2 issue is lead deduplication (12 calls, 26.7%) — customers called after already being contacted. When customers are receptive, the AI transfers successfully 100% of the time — the problem is upstream.
